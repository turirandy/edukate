Welcome to Edukate! We care about your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our website and services.

When you sign up or interact with our platform, we may collect the following information:

6. Children's Privacy

Edukate is intended for individuals aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child, we will delete it immediately.
